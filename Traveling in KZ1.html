Semey

Semey (Semipalatinsk in Russian) is often overlooked nowadays when people visit North Kazakhstan, thanks in part to the rise of immodest Astana.
That is too bad: where Astana points to the future of Kazakhstan, Semey is the embodiment of its past.Semey is one the most ancient cities of Kazakhstan.
Semey city is an important railway junction connecting Russia with Eastern and Southern Kazakhstan. Semey city has airport and river port.
Semey population is about 320 397 (2017 г.).
